# crawlerCarlDatasheet
A visual datasheet to accompany your Dungeon Crawler Carl experience.
The datasheet will let you easily track Carl's and his party's stats, items, skills, effects and more.

NOTE: The datasheet is spoiler protected. it has a chapter selection and will show the state of Carl's party at the beginning of the selected chapter.
